# Sima Berman Family Research Summary

Date: 2026-04-14  
Time: 10:46 Asia/Jerusalem

## Short Summary

We are researching the birth family of `Sima Berman`, who was born in `Dukla, Poland` on `24.12.1912`.

Local family documents clearly support:
- Sima's married name: `Sima Berman`
- maiden name used in records: `Krakowski`
- birth place: `Dukla, Poland`
- father: `Yitzhak-Meir`
- mother: `Malka`
- immigration to Palestine: `28.1.1935`

Other local documents probably support:
- an earlier or alternate surname: `Buxenboim` / `בוקסנבוים`
- a fuller form of the mother's name: `Margalit-Perl` / `מרגלית-פרל`

## What Family Testimony Says

According to cousin `Zohar`:
- Sima had `4 sisters`
- all `4 sisters` perished in the Holocaust
- both of Sima's parents also perished in the Holocaust

Important:
- this is currently treated as **family testimony**
- it has **not yet been fully confirmed** from documents or Yad Vashem

## What We Know for Sure

- Sima was born on `24.12.1912`
- Sima was from `Dukla, Poland`
- Sima later used the surname `Krakowski`
- Sima's father was `Yitzhak-Meir`
- Sima's mother appears as `Malka`
- Sima immigrated in `1935`
- Sima married `Shmuel Berman` in `1937`

## What We Think Is Probably True

- the family may also have used the surname `Buxenboim / Buxenbaum`
- the mother may have been called both:
  - `Malka`
  - `Margalit-Perl`
- Sima came from a larger family with several siblings
